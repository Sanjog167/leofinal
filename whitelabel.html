White Label FAQ

What is LeoSquad white label?

With LeoSquad's white label, you become your boss. The service is offered to you as a product by LeoSquad to be further rebranded with your name and logo and URLs.

For whom is LeoSquad's white-label suitable?

LeoSquad's white label is built keeping corporate houses in mind. LeoSquad's white label is best for those who want to rebrand under another company's brand or any existing company and get established.

Which payment methods can be offered?

Why would a Company want a LeoSquad white label?

There are several reasons your agency would want to consider LeoSquad's white label as this software is creating a brand value. With white label, you can enhance your company's brand and make people aware of how serious competitor you are. You can expand into a more potential market with your own name and add revenues. With your brand status, your company will hold a brighter market value and trust.

Is LeoSquad's white-label good enough to create a brand value?

There is no doubt about this. Your LeoSquad white label has a series of benefits that can keep your business up front and running. With LeoSquad White Label, you are benefited by following ways:

• Your business resources can be beneficially used with your own name, logo and URL.
• You can expand your rebranded product quickly, which gives you enough time to focus on your business.
• You can achieve every transaction in your name, you need not depend on others Bill.

How white labeling software works?

A white labeling software is like leasing and renting software to a buying company from another brand. Further, the buying company can rebrand the software as its own and establish a brand value to generate revenue from multiple platforms.
