Pricing FAQs

Can I use LeoSquad for free?

Absolutely! LeoSquad is completely free to use indefinitely with all the features you would need in a virtual office with a cap of 2 users in an account. If you are a startup trying to get off the ground, that’s another monthly bill you don’t have to worry about.

How does LeoSquad’s pricing work?

The free version of LeoSquad is available for teams of up to 2 users. LeoSquad’s Standard costs are divided into 4 sections Advance, Premium, Business and Enterprise (click here to view full pricing). When you switch to any Standard plan you can start a 14 day trial. And you will be charged only at the end of the 14 day trial.

Do I have to wait until the end of the trial to activate my plan?

NO. Just head over to the Billing tab side menu bar, and pay using any of our payment methods to activate the plan.

What happens when the trial period ends?

After your trial Period ends you will be reverted back to our Free plan allowing only 2 users to work in the system.

What happens when I add a 3rd member into my account?

You will not be allowed to save your details and ask to choose any standard plan before proceeding.

What payment method do you accept?

We currently accept NPS and esewa payment. You can also ask our representative for cheque or direct payment (click here to contact our representative)

How can I cancel my subscription?

If you wish to cancel your subscription you can go to the billing section and change your plan to free plan.

I don’t need all the features that LeoSquad offers. Do you price as per my requirement?

We customize the software for you and charge accordingly.
