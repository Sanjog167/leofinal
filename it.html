How teams use LeoSquad for Project Management Solution

Keeping the team on the same page.

Leosquad Project Management helps you organize and prioritize you and team members' work in manageable tasks. Easily view the project, deadline, priority, and even what time a task is due. Plus, use the easy filter to find any task in seconds and customize your lists to fit how you work.

Defining the Goals and Objectives.

Make a proper roadmap of your project. Plan your task and deadlines. Track your project record with a powerful kanban board and Gant chart in no time.

Delivering the project on time and budget.

Avoiding scope creep will increase the chance of delivering the project on time and within budget. With LeoSquad, you can make a clear outline of every step. Lock your requirement with Proper contract with our online contract Feature.

Measure Employee Productivity.

A team is as good as its team members. However, keeping an eye on every team is a hectic job. LeoSquad HR management, time tracking and milestone tracking helps you measure every member's KPI. Check employee attendance, leave, task status in a single software.

Fix Client issue on time.

Ticket tracking features provide teams to record the complete detailed report of complaints or bugs, helps everyone to understand the complete issue easily, prioritize the issue, and resolve it quickly.

Keeping an eye on your leads.

: CRM lead management and activity scheduler helps to manage your leads and clients scientifically. Additionally, the system allows access to much more enhanced features in client relation management.

Manage your finance.

Create instant bills, check payments, track expenses with finance dashboard. Keep an eye on the loopholes of your business economy with integrated features made to meet your financial support.

Stakeholder Involvement.

Stakeholders are your companion. LeoSquad provides a cloud-based system to make stakeholders updated with all of the companies’ status at instant with all reports in the dashboard. Besides, you can interact with your queries on the notification through your dashboard.
